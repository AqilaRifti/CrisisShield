## Voice & Tone

### Brand Voice
- **Professional** but approachable
- **Confident** but not arrogant
- **Empathetic** and understanding
- **Action-oriented** and practical
- **Optimistic** about solutions

### Tone Guidelines

**When discussing threats:**
- Be clear and direct
- Don't sensationalize
- Focus on preparation, not fear
- Provide actionable steps

**When discussing solutions:**
- Be confident and reassuring
- Emphasize empowerment
- Show concrete benefits
- Use success stories

**When addressing users:**
- Be respectful and supportive
- Acknowledge their challenges
- Celebrate their progress
- Encourage action

## Visual Style

### Photography
- **Real businesses:** Authentic, not stock photos
- **Diverse representation:** Various business types and regions
- **Positive framing:** Show resilience and recovery, not just destruction
- **Human-centered:** Focus on people, not just buildings

### Iconography
- **Simple and clear:** Easy to understand at a glance
- **Consistent style:** Rounded, friendly, modern
- **Purposeful:** Each icon should have clear meaning
- **Accessible:** High contrast, recognizable shapes

### UI Elements
- **Cards:** Rounded corners (8px), subtle shadows
- **Buttons:** Bold, clear CTAs with good contrast
- **Progress bars:** Smooth animations, clear percentages
- **Badges:** Color-coded for quick recognition
- **Alerts:** Appropriate severity colors

---

## Content Guidelines

### Headlines
- Start with action verbs
- Be specific and concrete
- Keep under 10 words
- Focus on benefits

**Good Examples:**
- "Predict Threats Before They Strike"
- "Generate Emergency Plans in Minutes"
- "Track Your Recovery Progress"

**Avoid:**
- Vague promises ("Be Better Prepared")
- Jargon ("Leverage AI for Risk Mitigation")
- Fear-mongering ("Don't Let Disaster Destroy You")

### Body Copy
- Use short paragraphs (2-3 sentences)
- Include bullet points for lists
- Bold key information
- End with clear next steps

### Call-to-Actions
- Be specific about what happens next
- Use action verbs
- Create urgency when appropriate
- Make buttons stand out

**Good CTAs:**
- "Get Your Free Threat Analysis"
- "Create Emergency Plan Now"
- "Start Protecting Your Business"
